## 🇬🇧 LinkedIn — English version

```
🧮 "How much tax do I actually pay as an Italian sole trader?"

I asked myself this every quarter. So I built Mastro: an open-source copilot for Italy's
"regime forfettario", running inside Claude (Claude Code). Install in 2 commands, your numbers
stay local, and every answer cites the law.

✅ /tasse (flat tax + social security + advances, deterministic) · /chiedi (cites the source,
or says "I don't know" instead of making it up) · /scadenze (deadlines) · /idoneo (never says
"you're eligible" — defers to your accountant).

💡 And I didn't do it alone. Mastro can cite sources because it stands on italia-corpus
(@ahmeabd) — ALL of Italian legislation from Normattiva, open, in Markdown, updated daily.
Together, something good happens: the law stops being experts-only. It's open (italia-corpus)
and finally queryable in plain language with the statute attached (Mastro). Tax law becoming
accessible to everyone.

🔧 /plugin marketplace add mikececco/mastro → /plugin install mastro@mastro-marketplace

🚧 Coming: an SRL (Ltd) edition, /confronta (forfettario vs SRL: when it's worth switching),
and a FatturaPA reader (point it at your e-invoice folder, get your live tax position).

⚠️ Beta (v0.2): forfettario verified against official sources, SRL still a skeleton. A help,
not a substitute for a tax advisor.

👉 github.com/mikececco/mastro   🙏 github.com/ahmeabd/italia-corpus
```
